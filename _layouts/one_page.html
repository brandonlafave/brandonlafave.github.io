About

My name is Brandon LaFave, and I'm a front-end developer who lives in Seattle. While working at Amazon, I was involved in several major launches, such as the opening of Kindle store in Netherlands and the release of Kindle Textbook Creator. I also initiated, created, and defined the media-query standards that Kindle teams throughout the world use for eBooks.

Portfolio

Bibliopile

Bibliopile allows users to search and borrow books from their friends' libraries. I completed it with three other developers under a four-day sprint. I used semantic HTML5, Sass, AJAX, JavaScript, and Angular. [More Info]

Kindle Textbook Creator

Kindle Textbook Creator is a free application for authors and educators to convert PDFs of textbooks, course notes, study guides, and other educational material into interactive Kindle books. I ensured quality assurance on the beta builds, provided UX feedback, and wrote the user guide. [More Info]
